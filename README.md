## 📖 About

**Sums Up** is a privacy-focused budget calculator that helps you plan and track your finances without ever creating an account or storing your data on someone else's server. Calculate, plan, and export your budget — all locally, all yours.

## ✨ Features

- 💰 **Pointed Budget Planning** — Build budgets that match your real-world expenses and goals
- 🔒 **Privacy First** — No accounts, no tracking, no data leaves your device
- 🌍 **Multi-Language Support** — Switch between supported languages on the fly
- 📂 **Export Your Work** — Save your budget in your preferred format
- 🖥️ **Cross-Platform** — Web, desktop, and (soon) mobile from a single codebase
- ⚡ **Lightweight** — Native desktop installer under 10 MB thanks to Tauri
